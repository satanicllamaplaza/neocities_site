Music for Fun, Not Profit

I used to be a professional musician and while it certianly had its ups, it had some heavy downs. I do not intent on going into too much detail. There was all the good hallmarks of a music career in Los Angeles, heavy substance abuse, long hours, pretentious artists, ect.

At some point I had a realization. I had completely stopped listening to music. If I wasn't working, I was listening to anything but music. During the last 10 years of my music creer I had written only 3 songs for myself, hundreds of songs for hire but only 3 that meant anything to me.

I closed my studio, sold all the gear and completely stopped writing music.

It took a few years before I found myself inspired to write again.

I would like to share some music with whoever arrives here. Some will be old files dug out of the drives of history and hopefully some of it will be new. I hope you enjoy whatever you find.
