Not an Artist

08.06.2024

I am no artist, not anymore, but I do make art!

This simple gallery will be a random smattering of pictures and drawings. I Used to be obsessed with lomography and even tried my hand at it for a while but it is a painfully expensive hobby. So I bought a programmable camera that really embodies the spirit of lomography. It has terrible quality, its highly unpredictable and I never know what I have till I get home.

With this camera I hope to program some of my own filters like a double exposure or an imbalance of color. I will talk about any filters I make in blog posts.

I may post a doodle or a song in here who knows. The world is my oyster... I just fucking hate oysters!
